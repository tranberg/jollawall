Help

Three easy steps to create an ambiance wallpaper for your Jolla:

  1. Hit the big green button to upload your image. Make sure its dimensions are at least 540×1600 pixels.
  2. After uploading a panel of zoom, crop and cancel buttons will appear in the top right corner. Drag and zoom your image until satisfied.
  3. After cropping a download link will be displayed at the bottom of the page.

About JollaWall

Having spent some time in Gimp creating ambiance wallpapers for my Jolla I thought it would be nice to have a fast and easy tool for doing so. I soon found Croppic, a jQuery plugin for cropping pictures, and then JollaWall was created.

My original inspiration comes from this site, which explains why the dimensions of the image should be 540×1600 pixels. I have previously made a package of wallpapers available here.

You can find the source on GitHub.
